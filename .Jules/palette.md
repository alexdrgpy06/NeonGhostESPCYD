## 2024-05-23 - Clearer Status Indicators on Embedded UI
**Learning:** On small, low-res displays (240x320), abstract symbols like `*` or `-` are confusing without context. Adding a dedicated legend row, even at the cost of vertical whitespace, significantly reduces cognitive load.
**Action:** When designing lists with status icons, prioritize finding space for a persistent legend or use universally recognized symbols (like Lock/Play) if custom icons aren't available.
