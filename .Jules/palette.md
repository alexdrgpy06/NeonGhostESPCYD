## 2026-03-14 - [Visual Feedback for Hardware Buttons]
**Learning:** [Immediate visual feedback on physical touchscreens is critical to prevent users from repeatedly tapping buttons, as hardware touch sensors lack the innate tactile feel of mechanical switches.]
**Action:** [Implement immediate color inversion state changes with a short delay directly in the touch handler before executing blocking or debounced logic to reassure the user their input was registered.]
