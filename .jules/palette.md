## 2024-04-24 - Touchscreen Button Feedback
**Learning:** On physical touchscreens, immediate visual feedback (like momentary color inversion with a short delay) is critical to prevent users from repeatedly tapping buttons, as they might not realize their tap registered.
**Action:** Implement momentary visual feedback for interactive buttons (e.g. FEED, ATTACK, MENU) by briefly redrawing the button with inverted foreground and background colors.
