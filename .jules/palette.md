## 2025-02-18 - Immediate TFT Touchscreen Feedback
**Learning:** Physical TFT touchscreens lack the tactile feedback of physical buttons. Without immediate visual confirmation, users may repeatedly tap buttons and trigger unintended multiple actions or state changes.
**Action:** When implementing interactive buttons on TFT screens, always provide immediate visual feedback (e.g., momentarily redrawing with inverted colors using a short delay, ~50ms) before processing the action.
