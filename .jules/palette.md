## 2024-11-20 - Physical Touchscreen Visual Feedback
**Learning:** Physical touchscreens lack tactile feedback. Without immediate visual confirmation when a button is pressed, users are likely to repeatedly tap buttons, causing unintended multiple actions or confusion.
**Action:** Always provide an immediate visual state change (like a momentary color inversion using a short delay, ~50ms) for main action buttons on physical touch interfaces to confirm the tap has been registered.
