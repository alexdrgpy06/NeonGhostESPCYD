## 2024-06-27 - Immediate visual feedback on touchscreens
**Learning:** Users on physical touchscreens tend to repeatedly tap buttons if there isn't immediate, noticeable visual feedback that their touch was registered. This can trigger multiple unintended actions.
**Action:** Always provide immediate visual feedback (e.g., momentarily redrawing the button with inverted colors or a "pressed" state using a short delay, ~50ms) on physical touchscreens to acknowledge the user's interaction instantly.
