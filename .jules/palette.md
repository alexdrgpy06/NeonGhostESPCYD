## 2024-05-24 - Hardware TFT Touchscreen Button Feedback
**Learning:** Users lack immediate tactile feedback on physical TFT touchscreens, causing them to repeatedly tap buttons when they think their input wasn't registered.
**Action:** Always provide immediate visual feedback (e.g., momentarily redrawing with inverted colors and a ~50ms delay) on hardware touchscreen interactions to confirm registration before executing the bound action.
