## 2024-07-01 - Immediate visual feedback on physical touchscreens
**Learning:** Physical touchscreens lack physical tactile feedback. Instant visual feedback (like momentarily inverting colors) is critical to prevent users from repeatedly tapping buttons, making the interface feel much more responsive and intuitive.
**Action:** Apply a short 50ms inverted color flash on all physical hardware touchscreen buttons upon interaction to guarantee input perceivability.
